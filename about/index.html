About us

Your neighbors with a truck and a mission

Mr Haul Hero is a locally owned junk removal company built on hard work, fair pricing, and giving back to the DFW community.

We started Mr Haul Hero with a simple idea: junk removal should be easy, honest, and actually helpful. No vague pricing, no no-shows, no junk left behind — just a friendly crew that shows up, does the heavy lifting, and leaves your space clean.

Every load we haul is a chance to do some good. We donate what we can to local charities, recycle everything possible, and give back to the community we're proud to call home. When you book Mr Haul Hero, you're not just clearing clutter — you're keeping usable items in the hands of people who need them and out of the landfill.
