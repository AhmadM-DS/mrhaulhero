FAQ

Common questions

What items do you take?
We haul almost anything non-hazardous: furniture, mattresses, appliances, electronics, yard debris, construction waste, and full cleanouts. We can't take hazardous materials like paint, chemicals, or asbestos — just ask if you're unsure.
How does pricing work?
Pricing is based on how much space your junk takes up in the truck and its weight. We provide a free on-site estimate before any work begins, so you always know the price upfront — no hidden fees.
Do you offer same-day service?
Yes, in most cases. We operate 24 hours, 7 days a week across DFW. Call or text early and we'll do our best to get to you the same day.
Are you licensed and insured?
Absolutely. Mr Haul Hero is fully licensed and insured, so your property and our crew are covered on every job.
What happens to the junk you remove?
We donate and recycle as much as we possibly can. Usable items go to local charities and recyclable materials are sorted out, keeping as much as possible out of the landfill.
